Chinese Dream Core: Turning Irretrievable Dreams into Fashion

Allison Lee

Navigation Menu

In that dreamscape of interwoven light and shadow, the blue-green glass gently swayed the time silhouette of the old-style residential building, and the white acrylic window frame outlined the mottled memories of the wall-sized tile. On the screen with noise and jitter, the old Windows computer silently stuck, and the blurred image hid the pure innocence that could not be returned. In the empty park, the hissing sound of the radio and the chorus of "Lubinghua" intertwined into a song, leading us to travel back to the millennium era, a time full of stories and dreams. Although all of this has gone, it has been given new life in the fashion wave of "Chinese Dream Core".

Dreamcore, originating from the early 21st century English art genre, blends surreal, fantasy, and subconscious elements. "Chinese Dreamcore" is a Chinese interpretation of this style. It skillfully integrates classic elements from the millennial era, such as old-fashioned televisions and wooden furniture, with high saturation and mottled blur filters, creating a familiar yet dreamlike atmosphere. 

As dreamlike and illusory as they are, these elements are not only able to stay in photos and videos, but also can be integrated into our daily wear and become a beautiful scenery on the streets. Here are some references for mixing Chinese dreamcore style and modern fashion trends, which you can use for reference.

The high-saturation Barbie pink tank top, paired with a grey worn-out texture low-waisted denim A-line skirt, recreated the youthful style of the millennium. Looking back at old photos from the millennium, it is difficult not to notice the popularity of low-waisted bottoms, and women on the street confidently show off their beautiful waistlines. In addition, old photos also cannot hide the youthful style conveyed by the bright tops popular in the millennium. This outfit perfectly replicates the youthful atmosphere of the millennium, suitable for weekend trips with friends or summer music festivals. The low-waisted design shows off a confident waistline, while the Barbie pink top becomes the focus of the entire scene, allowing you to boldly show off your personality.

A blue mesh dress with thin straps, paired with long white workwear jeans. High-saturation blue glass is a popular building material in China's 90s style, and is a common element in Chinese dreamcore photos. It is often used to match the white square brick exterior walls, which are common in both rural and urban areas in China. This outfit replicates the style of houses from that era by using similar colors.

Retro grotesque animation print loose t-shirt, with white bordered sports shorts in a deep blue color. The memory of childhood playgrounds is turned into prints to wear on the body, paired with dark blue sports shorts, which is both retro and full of childlike fun. This outfit is suitable for leisure time or outdoor activities, allowing you to express your personality in a relaxed and comfortable way. The dark blue ankle-length sports pants are a reminder of school uniform pants for many Chinese people. This outfit uses the same color shorts for matching, which is more versatile and adapts to more people's body shapes.

A white Polo shirt with a black and pink striped cake skirt reveals sweetness and vitality in a simple style. A white sports baseball cap adds a casual feel, and a wavy high ponytail shows youthful vitality. This outfit combines the retro style of the millennial generation with modern fashion, making it suitable for campus strolls or sports moments, showing unique personality and charm.

A rainbow-colored knitted cardigan is paired with a white strapless dress and a pair of colorful striped canvas shoes. Rainbow colors always easily awaken people's yearning for a better life, and this knitted cardigan instantly lights up the entire look with its gorgeous colors. The white strapless dress serves as an inner layer, simple yet fresh, forming a sharp contrast with the rainbow-colored cardigan, yet harmonious and unified. The colorful striped canvas shoes at the feet echo the cardigan, and the overall look is full of childlike joy and vitality, as if walking on a rainbow, and the mood is brightened accordingly.
As the wheel of time rolls forward, people begin to miss those simple and pure days. Chinese Dream Core is the product of such a nostalgia trend. It skillfully combines classic elements of the millennial era with modern aesthetics, creating a unique style that is both retro and fashionable. Old-fashioned TV sets, wooden furniture, yellowed old photos... These seemingly ordinary objects, under the interpretation of Chinese Dream Core, radiate new vitality, making people feel as if they have traveled back in time to an era full of warmth and dreams.
The popularity of Chinese dreamcore is closely related to its deep penetration into the psychological needs of contemporary people. In fast-paced modern life, people often feel tired and anxious, while Chinese dreamcore provides a spiritual haven with its unique nostalgic atmosphere and dreamy temperament. At the same time, it also satisfies people's aesthetic pursuit for novel and unique artistic styles, becoming an important way for contemporary people to express their personality and attitude. Let us use the name of fashion to turn those unreturnable dreams into a bright color in daily life and continue to move forward on the road of pursuing dreams.
